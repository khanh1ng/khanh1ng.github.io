This is where I publish write-ups of my quantitative research: strategies I've backtested, models I've built, and markets I trade or study.

A few ground rules I hold myself to here:

1. **Out-of-sample or it didn't happen.** Every result gets labeled: in-sample, out-of-sample, or live.
2. **Show the graveyard.** Failed factors and dead strategies are documented alongside survivors — the base rate matters.
3. **No unverifiable numbers.** If I cite a Sharpe or a drawdown, the methodology is in the post or linked from it.
